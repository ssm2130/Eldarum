Eldarum Atlas

Continental Atlas

Seven continents structure Eldarum. Each hub lists anchored regions—breadcrumbs, the header link bar, and the sidebar show where you are and where you can leap next.

Granular settlement write-ups live in this atlas and the Northwake gazetteer unless a region has its own article.

This atlas balances two truths: your table has invested years in Asceveron’s Northwake (Stammont, Zrelia, the frontier march, Zehas, Collimar, Towfort, Kazon routes), and the world is larger than any single frontier. Other continents widen trade winds, Titan strata, and imperial habits—and they borrow narrative oxygen without erasing local stories.

Start from World of Eldarum for ocean layout and how the seven plates meet; use Calendar, weather & trade when you need in-universe weeks, seasons, and convoy timing that match the Northwake campaign year.
