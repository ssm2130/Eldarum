Overview

Odesus dries southward into dune seas, salt flats that remember oceans, and kiln cities where brass gates burn tourists’ fingerprints honest. Water rights fuel wars lighter than air and heavier than registries—inheritance flows through wells before bloodlines.

For continental placement and neighboring theaters, read World of Eldarum; for sun-season reckoning and caravan calendars, see Calendar, weather & trade.

Qalam basin cities

Stepwell palaces ring poetry duels judged by echo—losers donate irrigation rights; winners donate humility until the next drought.

Glass deserts

Titan Wars fused sand into blade-edged mirrors—prospectors mine “war glass” for lenses revealing invisible ink or guilt depending on moon bias. Merchants swear oaths on shards that cut palms when lies trade hands.

Sunforge anvil dome

A Forge Twins cathedral smelts dawn into ingots—blacksmith pilgrims argue theology with hammers louder than Choir permits.

Mirage courts

Judges wear veils thicker than law—Cases conclude when heatstroke hallucinations align across three witnesses.

Azure sink harbors

Ports occupy sinkholes where ships descend politely—cranes powered by oath-water tension.

Solar faith economies

Mirror Sovereign shrines sell reflective tiles said to dupe scorpion spirits; Silent Arbiter monks trek with sealed masks to avoid sympathizing with thirsty ghosts.
