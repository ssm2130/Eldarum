Overview

Trukuma rolls east in grass tides and black-soil braids—a continent of motion rather than fenceposts. Khanates bloom between festivals; borders persist until thaw turns ink into hospitality. Caravans from Stammont hire Trukuma navigators when crown maps pretend rivers behave; Zrelian admirals quietly budget for the same expertise because stars outrank admiralty pins once you lose sight of smoke.

Shard-fall smiths talk like Odesian glass-miners but swap desert sun for meteor choir—both trades inherited Titan War debris logic described in Titans deep lore. Continental facts here follow atlas-tier canon: geography and culture on this page yield to Pantheon manuscripts only when cosmology, not grasslands, is what changed.

For how Trukuma connects to the wider ocean ring and fellow continents, read World of Eldarum; for seasonal fairs and trek calendars, see Calendar, weather & trade.
