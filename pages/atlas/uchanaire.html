Overview

Uchanaire eats cartographers for brunch. Canopies fold sunlight into green libraries; rivers climb cliffs on roots trained longer than dynasties. Nations weigh wealth in seeds, venom catalogs, and callable favors from beings older than taxonomy.

For continental context south of Asceveron and Lucani, read World of Eldarum; for wet-season trade and river-market weeks, see Calendar, weather & trade.

Ladder towns

Vertical streets argue about altitude—lawsuits ascend until judges require rope qualifications.

Poison perfume houses

Assassins and aesthetes share consultants—fragrance labeled “evening certainty” may be diplomacy or homicide; customs sniffer bees unionized.

River root cathedrals

Sanctuary roots drink theology straight from water—Silent Arbiter pilgrims seal masks not against plague but empathy: spirits remember mourners’ faces too well.

Verdant necropolis

Tombs bloom orchids fertilized by epitaph quality—bad poets weed themselves.

Trade northward

Spice runs north; steel runs south; scholars run out of adjectives. Asceveron rumor claims certain contraband perfumes disguise prophecies—dogs sneeze in hexameter.
