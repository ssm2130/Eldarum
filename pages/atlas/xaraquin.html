Overview

Xaraquin occupies Eldarum’s crown of cold—not a single empire but braided principalities sworn to clarity. Summers borrow sunlight on credit; winters collect with interest. Travelers from warmer continents mistake stillness for emptiness until they hear glaciers crack like verdicts.

For polar placement within Eldarum’s oceans and ice routes to warmer ports, read World of Eldarum; for twilight-season reckoning and sled-trade windows, see Calendar, weather & trade.

Aurora archives

Libraries capture auroral grammar in braided wire—historians insist some curtains spell repeal for laws signed under false stars. Skeptics call it pareidolia; priests sell telescopes anyway.

Glass fjords

Fjords hold Titan-fired glass drowned and polished by ages—pilgrims skate sermons scratched into luminous sheets. Ships navigate by timbre when fog steals color.

Cordilleran observatories

Mountain temples chart moral alignments like weather: Iocetis tides brush certain peaks cleaner than others. Visiting scholars swap notebooks; local kings swap astrologers like hostages dressed as ornaments.

Ice-throne diplomacy

Frost courts convene briefly—delegates swear on thawing crowns that reform each season. Rumor claims hymns to Halzatra hide in avalanche harmonics; nobody agrees which slope argues fairly.
