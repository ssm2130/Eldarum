Relics of the Scourge

These artifacts are framed in-setting as tied to the Scourge—the catastrophic Titan cascade described in faith lore. Many use Dormant / Awakened / Exalted (or similar) progression. Campaign rule: a creature may be attuned to only one Relic of the Scourge at a time unless a specific entry says otherwise. Attunement is often taxing (commonly a DC 18 Constitution save on attune, with exhaustion risk—see journal).

Dormant, Awakened & Exalted

Many Relics unlock features across tiers—exact DCs, charges, and story unlocks are only in the full archive. In-world, sages blame shard resonance and the same Scourge geology that made Echo Titans unstable.

Named arms & sentient weapons

  • Night's Edge (Svlthralryn) — sentient greatsword; Astral dreadnought soul shard; high mental stats; hearing and darkvision for the weapon sense.
  • Rak Bal'uroth — see table; infernal craft and Titan shard pommel.
  • Pyrravyn — multi-piece greatsword questline tied to Zrelia and the Plane of Fire.

Spellbooks & arcana

  • Infinitous Codex — grimoire Relic; competes for the single-Relic attunement slot with other Scourge artifacts.
  • Abrakary — not one item but a lost arcane tradition predating Magi-Bane; individual spells appear in handouts (Chicanerous Semblance, Pensive Dissolution, Replenishment, Steadfast Object, Planar Void, etc.). See campaign hub — Magic & lore.
