Great Road & transitive planes

Travelers speak of a Great Road — not one highway but a braid of stable transitions: stories that become geography (Feywild), silver arguments that become charts (Astral), and the bruise-colored commute ghosts prefer (Ethereal). Each plane below can be read beside the Underworld for contrast — decay versus story, silver versus rot.

Material Plane

Overview. Eldarum’s ordinary reality — where crowns pay for grain and Titan scars still ache. Nothing is “neutral” here; geography holds grudges.

TraitNotes
Alignment tiltNone intrinsic — morality is mortal theater staged on contested ground
Gravity / timeBaseline physics; calendars are political
MagicAll schools function; genius loci and relic-law distort locally

Environment. Continents (see atlas hub), seas such as Greywake, Scar terrain where planar bleed pooled after Titan deaths.

Inhabitants. Everyone you’ve played for years.

Travel hooks. Every portal problem begins as a customs problem.

Feywild

Overview. Story-heavy wildness leaking sideways into nature — treaties age in dog years; oaths sprout metaphorical thorns.

TraitNotes
AlignmentCapricious — beauty and hazard share costumes
Gravity / timeBends around narrative beats; dusk can last a court session
MagicEnchantment and glamour rewarded; iron stubbornness varies by court

Environment. Oversaturated biomes — flowers shout color; rivers reroute when insulted.

Inhabitants. Archfey audiences, borrowed saints, beasts that copyright their own footprints.

Psychological pressure. Visitors bargain without noticing — identity theft via compliments.

Astral Sea

Overview. Silver tides where thought becomes topography; gods moor campaigns like fleets.

TraitNotes
GravityOptional — determination doubles as locomotion
TimeMeasured in arguments resolved
MagicPsychic and planar anchors dominate; stray thoughts leave wakes

Environment. Silver clouds, shattered dominions drifting like icebergs, highways of covenant light.

Hazards. Gith wars echo forever; forgotten prayers orbit like debris.

Ethereal Veil

Overview. Border state touching everything — ghosts commute before deciding mood; haunts rehearse unfinished geometry.

TraitNotes
SensationMuted palette; sounds arrive late
TransitBleeds into Border Ethereal near emotional wounds and Titan strata
MagicNecromancy and phase spells resonate; walls remember footsteps

Psychological pressure. Visitors confuse memory with rehearsal — déjà vu weaponized.

Elemental commons

Four “neutral” elemental planes campaign texts treat as shared quarries — none are polite tourist stops.

Water

Ocean without shore — pressure as philosophy. Ships made of choral vowels; storms file patents.

Air

Sky without ground — lightning writes temporary law; birds testify in gusts.

Fire

Forges without accountants — pleasure and danger share tongues; salamanders unionize ironically.

Earth

Root-depth archives — mountains negotiate slowly; dwarf highways are polite fiction.

Upper moral planes — Good alignments

Each realm pairs a moral shade with a patron impulse — not “nice vacation spots” but argument engines where virtue has paperwork.

Aenimar (Chaotic Good)

Freedom-with-conscience; vows optional but infectious. Courts sponsored by hurricane philanthropists.

Yasslyn (Neutral Good)

Consistency without theater — hospitals that refuse drama yet charge attention.

Iocetis (Lawful Good)

Measured mercy — statutes warm to the touch; mercy indexed by precedent.

Lower moral planes — Evil alignments

Sibling courts to the upper tier — recruitment pitches differ; appetite does not.

Uccasos (Chaotic Evil)

Invention without remorse — storms enjoy witness; laboratories grow teeth.

Dritora (Neutral Evil)

Debt as sacrament — patience sharper than swords; interest accrues in dreams.

Zaezirus (Lawful Evil)

Chains measured to fit souls — audits eternal; mercy scheduled next epoch.
