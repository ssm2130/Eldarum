Great Road planes

  • Material — ordinary reality where campaigns pay rent.
  • Feywild — story-heavy wildness leaking sideways into nature; treaties age in dog years.
  • Underworld — jurisdiction of endings, courts, and things that dislike sky.
  • Astral — silver tides, silver lies, geography as argument.
  • Ethereal — veil-state bordering everything; ghosts commute here before deciding mood.

Underworld — mood & bleed

In Eldarum, Titan War entropy pooled into Scar geography — places where decay negotiates with cartography. Underworld doctrine borrows from older courts: reflections that misbehave, rot as infrastructure, silence as terrain.

For the long-form gazetteer (traits, natives, mirrored cities, throne politics, story hooks) start at The Underworld — Eldarum cosmology. Cross-read with Titan Wars, Titans deep lore, and Eldarum Titans primer.
