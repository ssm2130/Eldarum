What is a Titan?

A Titan is:

  • A fully manifested fragment of divine essence
  • Anchored in the Material Plane during the Celestial Alignment
  • Sustained by outer planar energy leakage

They are not mere servants — they are semi-autonomous, capable of independent strategy, and embodiments of pure domain expression.

Why Titans appeared

During the Celestial Alignment the veil thinned; outer planes bled into reality; gods could exert direct influence. Yet full divine manifestation would destroy ordinary physics. Powers forged Titans as controlled vessels — continent-sized arguments that could strike without vaporizing the negotiation table.

Titan classification system

1. Prime Titans (god-direct avatars)

Mountain-scale combatants channeling patrons directly — intellect godlike, power drawn through sustained divine links. Their deaths throw off major shards — the ransom empires levy on each other.

2. Echo Titans

Residual manifestations when Primes shattered — unstable, region-bound, excellent boss-tier threats.

3. Shardbound Titans

Mortal meddling with shards — rituals, fusion, ambition — produces artificial Titans with wild power swings.

Named Prime engines (examples)

Verdant Cataclysm (Nila’s Titan)

A living storm of roots, stone, and lightning that could overwrite terrain instantly. Critical insight: Nila did not deploy a single avatar — she synchronized multiple Titans into one coordinated surge, ending the open war phase and seeding the Scourge.

The Iron Arbiter (Iocetis-aligned)

Construct-like colossus of law magic — attempted battlefield stabilization; where it succeeded, time sometimes froze in strata (legal absurdities mortals still mine as precedent).

The Chaos Maw (Uccasos-aligned)

Ever-mutating engine rewriting its own anatomy — useful thematic bridge to moral-plane doctrine for Uccasos.

Scourge & aftermath

When synchronized strikes forced multiple Primes to annihilate together, chained divine energy fractured reality — shards, planar scars, unstable regions Odesus prospectors still exploit. Tie this entropy bleed to Underworld-adjacent geography and lower moral planes.
