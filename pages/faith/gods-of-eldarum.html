Halzatra — evil umbrella

The ledger lists Halzatra as “The Dark Lord,” evil alignment, holding every evil domain in aggregate (parallel to Melandor’s good mantle). Cultists treat named fiends below as facets—never as “lesser,” only as specialized invoices.
