Scope & editorial choices

This archive prioritizes Eldarum-native canon. Materials that wandered in from other worlds (for example alien “Bane” treatises copied by mistake into early notebooks) are not reproduced here. Site-wide priority rules for conflicting pages live at Lore & primary sources.

For Ithar, only Eldarum theology appears in the directory — annihilation of meaning and consequence, never imported cosmologies.

Liturgical languages: dragonholds, dwarf-septs, and elven choruses translate epithets and drum cadences differently, but the timeline and cast match the Common chronicle; variance is regional voice, not competing physics.
