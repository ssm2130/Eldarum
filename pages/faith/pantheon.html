Why veils exist

Theology across Eldarum agrees on one inconvenient truth: gods are too large for single names. Temples distribute aspects — Storm Lord as judge, Storm Lord as harbor bell — knowing parishioners will simplify anyway. Veils also protect mortals from symmetry: seeing the whole pattern can convert faith into paralysis. For spreadsheet-aligned names and planes, see Gods of Eldarum.

Titans primer tie-ins

Nila (growth & ruin). Field doctrine ties her to the Verdant Cataclysm — synchronized Prime Titans rewriting terrain mid-war. Hedge theologians insist her gift was multiplicity, not excess body count.

Iocetis (LG) & the Iron Arbiter. Law gods sponsored construct-theology Titans — crystalline statutes made march. Court libraries in Cilobria cite frozen-time pockets as binding precedent.

Uccasos (CE) & the Chaos Maw. Entropic evangelists celebrate impossible anatomy; diplomats call it weaponized improvisation. Either way, planar scholars map its route onto old battle scars near Odesus.

Storm Lord

Domains: judgment, squalls, coastal bells, oaths shouted into wind.

Practices: chapels on headlands; confession delivered facing gales; copper pins bent into lightning vectors to signal interrupted pride. In Asceveron, adherents investigate how divine power routes through local shrines when crowns don’t hold the deed — politics by lightning rod.

Allies & frictions: respects Forge Twins’ craft pacts; argues with Mirror Sovereign over espionage sanctified as weather.

Forge Twins

Domains: invention, repair, merciful heat, catastrophic heat.

Practices: paired anvils in guildhalls — one twin accepts broken tools, one accepts broken promises. Odesus’s Sunforge Dome elevates solar forge theology; Cilobrian metric glassworks borrow ritual language without licensing soul.

Silent Arbiter

Domains: death, archives, debts, endings that refuse theater.

Practices: monks scratch palimpsests rather than speak aloud in shrine vaults; contracts in Uchanaire sometimes sealed with Arbiter wax that stains if amended dishonestly.

Mirror Sovereign

Domains: illusion, diplomacy, espionage, reversible identities.

Practices: masked festivals in Zrelian harbors; “listening rooms” in Baycliff sell reflections that exaggerate virtues — taxed as cosmetics.

Bramble Martyr

Domains: healing through painful growth — herbs, surgery, bitter truths.

Practices: hedge hospitals along war fronts; initiation scars patterned like briar — cosmetic in Cilobria, survival elsewhere.

Liturgical calendar tensions

When Storm holy days collide with Forge furnace festivals, cities schedule “echo services” — duplicate bells so both truths feel attended. Critics call it polytheist bookkeeping; priests call it survival.
