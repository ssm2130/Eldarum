Sverge Republic

Canal grids, charter courts, and southern seas that finance whatever story the tide tells.

Sverge carries the same civic metabolism as Cilobria—Senate cousins measure distance in canal-hours and justice in precedent stacks—but salt air keeps its temperaments louder. Guild barges ship metric cloth north while southern harbors negotiate seasonal piracy as weather: emboldened squadrons south of the republic send ransom notes ahead of rum, and admiralty clerks learn to read smoke on the horizon as punctuation.

Interior reform sermons sometimes veil older hungers. Field briefs name Dortar Eterna recruitment drives in southern cities—street charities with ledger ink that never dries—where Malvolia Sarka and Dhotarr rumor overlap; those arcs thread through planning summaries on the Campaign journal rather than this gazetteer map alone.
