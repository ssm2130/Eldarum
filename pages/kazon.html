Kazon

Island entrepôt where charters fray and destinies dock.

Kazon rises from turquoise shallows crowned with mismatched architecture—Stammont stone beside Zrelian iron lace beside timber stolen from nobody asking. Neutral in treaty ink but rarely in practice, the port sells jurisdiction alongside oranges. Expeditions embark here toward volcanic arcs and Titan-haunted ridges rumored inland.

Governance

A Syndicate of Harbormasters rotates annually; bribery is taxed formally as “expedition facilitation.” Pirates visiting under truce flags pay convenience tithes; temples argue whether coin washed in seawater counts as purified.

Greywake economics — neutrality as performance

Kazon sells neutral water the way theaters sell tragedy: everyone knows Stammont tariffs and Zrelian admiralties subsidize different wings of the same auction house. Expeditions stage here before threading volcanic arcs toward Zehas or island chains described in Lucani atlas plates.

Session-grade facts—who docked which contraband on which date—live in the campaign archive; cosmology stays aligned with Pantheon Tier 1 per our canon stack.
