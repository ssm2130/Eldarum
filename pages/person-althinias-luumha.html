Althinias Luumha

Field name of a bounty hunter trained among Vanguardians—justice priced in league with Maesis's protectors.

Sailors along Sverge's eastern rocks whisper of bronze wings and freak weather; inland warrants know only Althinias Luumha, the polite young elf who reads ledgers of fugitives like scripture and rarely loses a trail once coin and conscience align.

Crushing Depths

Born from an egg mishandled by fate: a corrupted clutch-mate abandoned on a peak, later stolen into Crushing Depths cult architecture under Malvolia Sarka's ambitions. Hatchling trauma among Dhotarr-adjacent rites ended when infiltrators breached the temple—Malvolia fled minus two fingers; the child found rescue with Iolas Elrel, who carried him toward sanctuary in Highrock among Maesis-aligned monks (the Vanguardians).

Vanguardians

Renamed Bevaltvax—the redeemed—and trained as both arcane knight and man-hunter, Althinias accepted a wandering identity that reads as Iolas's kin while Maesis's oath keeps Asceveron's predators on ledgers. Shard-resonance in his blood accelerated growth and hunger alike: coin sunk from wrecks funds a hoard meant not for vanity but for wars against tyranny.

Crossed paths

Orders placed him on the trail of Grax Brelon, pyrate killer tied to Captain Brellon's wreckage off Plailes; rumor drew him through Roafort toward Greenvail, where patrol gossip merged with Grand Master O'Hannon's errands. Campaign briefings still layer Dhotarr hunts atop personal reckoning—Malvolia's ledger and Malvolia's smile are not two separate storms.
