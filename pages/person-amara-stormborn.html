Amara Stormborn

Storm League courier—cleanse, renew, and never let thunder grow idle.

Amara carries a mandate older than crowns: keep Toros's storms potent enough to wash rot from coasts and crowns alike. Where other faithful pray for calm harbors, her circle prays that rain remembers how to scour.

Vows

The Storm League treats Toros not as a distant patron but as the hydraulic engine of Eldarum's renewal—storms that smash fleets also refill aquifers; lightning that splits masts also splits lies open in court. Amara moves with that paradox: destructive and generative in the same breath. Nila's servants, in League teaching, call on Toros as the equalizer when nature and empire argue in the same sky.

Road north

Her path began on Odesus in Pututa'ane, then by sea through Lucani (Kituwai, Silae, Jala) to Sverge's capital Narlow, north to Hazelshire, and across the straits to Baycliff in Stammont. From there she aimed northwest toward Asceveron's Greenvale Forest, pausing in Beggars Hole within the Elliswich Quag—where Brothi, a lizardfolk Storm Leaguer, warned her off solo travel and pointed her toward fellow travelers already bound north.

Current brief

Rumors reached the League that power is coalescing in Asceveron stronger than elsewhere; she is to observe, judge, and if need be invoke Toros so nature—not any single throne—sets the tempo. Seasoned field notes also task her with finding the wandering druid Gadarast, beginning in the north and working south toward Faehodin.
