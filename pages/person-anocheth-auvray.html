Anocheth Auvray

Merchant Navy posting—tables know him in port chatter as Motjaba.

Narlow raised him between ledger ink and harbor bells: father Thommas captains for the Merchant Navy; mother Amabillia keeps a quiet house; elder brother Bellus smiths; sister Adela counts crates for Long Trading—the republic's highest-ranked trading house.

Smoke and mirrors

Strong emotion once unraveled Anocheth's face into stranger contours wreathed in black vapor—a family shame handled with silence until navy mentors reframed it as asset. Specialty training followed: a year sailing under Long Trading colors, two in uniform, then cloaked work coordinated with Admiral Garelet Bouchet's office. Only the family and his handler fully understand the smoke; foreign postings assume disguise kits and layered cloaks explain every inconsistency.

Brief abroad

Crown commerce in Stammont, dynasty lanes in Zrelia, and Greywake hull counts sit in the same portfolio—know where grain flows before admiralties do. Friendship with Remi Forlette persists wherever Sverge hulls share a pier.
