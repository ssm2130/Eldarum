Fiadh Stormforge

Blood hunter raised by the Order of the Lycan— tusks worn for memory, blades for answers.

Half-orc by blood, wholly Pack by oath: Fiadh trains to hunt what common folk fear, carrying her mother's tusks as jewelry because the tribe never gave her a funeral she could attend.

Castle Ghealach

The Black Tusk claimed fogged valleys from mountain to Rockcoast surf. Chieftain Nok'tar aged into contested leadership; shaman Lag'ra loved a human trader, Rolland, despite Khartag's ambitions. The night coup broke both lovers—Rolland fled with an infant while Khartag's hunters dragged him to a ravine. Mentor Darragh Stormforge, duergar brother-in-arms to Rolland, tore the child from lycanthropic grief, named her Fiadh for tempest spirit, and raised her inside the Order beside Terra, Dante, and their daughter Charolind.

Trials

Order healers bound her curse until discipline caught up with instinct; Darragh forged tusks into earrings when she came of age and promises Rolland's bow when trials end. Rumor claims Darragh hunts the disinherited father across dive bars and mercenary gigs—Fiadh now walks the march to learn whether mentor or sire still breathes.
