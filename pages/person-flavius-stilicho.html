Flavius Stilicho

Arcane chassis walking Stammont roads—court fiction says “artificer courier”; astrologers mutter stranger genesis.

Kaliphate workshops under the Zrelian dynasty once forged humanlike frames to slip past border scripts. Flavius is the rare line that stayed operational: a soul-vein wired into a crystalline core, will bent toward Hazoret's Pharaoh-cult and whatever orders arrive through smoke-encrypted channels.

Shard pulse

His continued motion depends on a red shard medallion—a power cell no court healer admits to recognizing. Without it, the body powers down within a minute; artificer rigging may someday internalize the matrix, but for now the amulet is both life support and tell. Restored cognition comes in layers: first motor function, then tradecraft, then the uneasy clarity that Stammont was always the assignment.

Dynasty angles

Hazoret's mandate and Zrelian strategists align on intelligence: map garrisons along Darkshore, Rockcoast, Slygallow; seed unease where crowns look brittle; gauge Sverge dependence on Lucani hulls. What the chassis remembers versus what it admits in taverns are two campaigns. Cross-read Zrelia, Hazoret hints if your table elevates him beyond folk saint, and Player characters.
