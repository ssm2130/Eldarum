Grax Brelon

Survivor of Captain Brellon's banner—crocodile patience, navy grudges, appetite for verdicts.

Raised as a pet trophy on a swamp isle raid, Grax learned softskin manners the way barracudas learn hull lines: slowly, then all at once. Brellon treated him like a siege engine with a heartbeat; the crew treated him like a rumor that bites.

Brellon's theater

Pirates played the lizardfolk's hunger for theater: prisoners handed rings back on severed hands, screams sold as deterrent, grain convoys hijacked until Greywake ballads claimed Brellon fed Dragon's Tooth Lagoon when mainland silos ran dry—never charity, always leverage, yet the settlement remembered the grain. Royal squadrons finally trapped the raider alongside a prize hull; flame and Volley drowned Brellon even as Grax dragged him toward shore. Ash taught aversion to fire; blue-coat victories taught a colder diet.

Hunters' convergence

Sverge warrants and Vanguardian ledgers both mark Grax—one for pyrating, another for answering to Brellon's corpse—while buried chests remain his captain's leverage even in death. Follow currency here through Althinias, Kazon lanes, and Player characters.
