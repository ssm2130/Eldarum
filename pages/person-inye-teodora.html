Inye (Teodora)

Scholar of Dukbini—Ala's bargain turned her longevity into a shard-haunted expedition.

The Dabu herd raised her in Ubaba's communal wisdom until Totter Rot nearly wrote her obituary. Rain washed a red crystal from the soil; she woke remade. Decades at the Dapany Institute under Musa Adeyemi earned the title Scholar of Dapany—and still mortality whispered.

Ala

Patron visions framed shard lore as the escape hatch: god-war detritus concentrated in Asceveron's north, especially where battle-scarred earth became swamp. Inye forged Inye's Death Wave—a cantrip shell of spirits—to walk dangerous roads. When she finally quit the libraries, Musa gifted coin and a shard amulet already catalogued in institute vaults.

Road to Elliswich

Sixteen months of ships and dust: Xaraquin's Dukbini to Lucani's Komsom and Jala; Chotoise and inland Sverge hops; Roafort papers arranged through Lord Alan Neylan after Crimson Guard scrutiny; then Towfort-ward toward the Elliswich Quag Godswar scar she sought—Greenvail's ancient battlefield mud.
