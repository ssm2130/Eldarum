Skudana

Acolyte who reads curses like bad weather reports.

Encountered in Collimar’s arcade hospitals, Skudana threads the line between parish helper and scholarly diagnostician—identifying ailments that resist physic, prayer, or palatable tea. Veterans with inexplicable exhaustion sometimes receive referral parchments bearing Skudana’s cramped script: warnings that only after cataloging a curse might a cure become thinkable—barring miracles most temples price beyond common purses.

Expertise

Lexicons of ancient malisons, comparative mythology mapping symptoms to saga fragments, discreet contacts with archivists who should not admit they keep bottles of stranger things. Skudana discourages heroic self-martyrdom unless patients sign waivers poetic enough for courts.

Where the story bends

When campaigns chase royal wedding plagues, relic contamination, or Julian’s slow cure, Skudana becomes the NPC who translates wonder into paperwork—linking ward drama to the Campaign journal without contradicting relic stats locked in the Roll20 archive.
