Obsidian Order cartographer—silver hair, astral syllables, vault ink on her palms.

Raised in Collimar's respectable middle tiers, Vishra occupied the overlap between librarian calm and spirit weather: pale skin that catches lamplight like porcelain, eyes intensifying toward blue flame as ki fights quori tides inside her skull.
